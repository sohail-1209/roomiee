--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- ZERO BROKERAGE: We save users up to two months of rent on deposits and commissions 
  by facilitating 100% direct tenant-to-owner interactions.
- REAL-TIME PRESENCE CHATS: Built-in instant messaging with typing indicators, 
  read receipts, and live online/offline/last-seen status to make coordination 
  instantaneous and reliable.
- AI INTELLIGENT SEARCH: A natural-language visual query engine. Users can type 
  sentences like "cozy room under ₹8000 near tech park with high-speed wifi" and 
  get hyper-accurate matching listings instantly.
- VERIFIED OWNER & HOSTEL PROFILES: Profiles and listings undergo rigorous validation 
  to build complete trust and weed out fake listings or scammers.
- IMMERSIVE MAP & GRAPHICS: Interactive location pickers and image galleries to
